Network

Rede Optimus is a European Research Organization, specialized in the Cardiovascular Field.

One of the strengths of RO is its large network of experienced investigators and high-volume sites.
Furthermore, the involvement of Key Opinion Leaders in the field of cardiovascular medicine and experienced clinical trial project managers ensure a perfect base for good clinical data. RO studies are currently beeing performed in Europe, UK and Brazil
